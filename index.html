Canyon AI Research Lab

About Us

CAIR's mission is to give students the best environment possible to learn and collaborate on AI-related endeavors. Our goal is to create an atmosphere that fosters personal research and also encourages collaborative efforts, allowing our members to network, explore, and innovate together within the realm of AI.

General

Our workshops are open to all students interested in AI, offering a welcoming environment for learning and exploration. However, club meetings are specifically dedicated to working on ongoing projects. To actively participate in these projects, members must first undergo an interview process with the project leader to ensure their skills align with project needs. This ensures productive and effective collaboration.

Members have complimentary access to GPUs for club projects and can use these resources for personal projects for a nominal fee of $20 per semester, supporting resource maintenance. For more information or to attend a workshop, please contact our team.
